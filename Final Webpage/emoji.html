Emoji Project

These are some custom emojis I made using Illustrator CC. The top-left one is inspired by Kamina's glasses from the anime Gurren Lagann. The top-right one is inspired by the color pattern of a bicolor cat. The bottom-left one was inspired by the "anxious with sweat" and "grimacing" emojis to make a sort of "oops" emoji.
